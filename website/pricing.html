Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each plan?

All plans include access to the core features: voter management, candidate management, event scheduling, call tracking, and pledge management. Higher tiers include additional features like agent management, real-time messaging, and advanced reporting.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected immediately, and billing will be adjusted accordingly.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Basic and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may include setup and customization fees depending on your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and other local payment methods. Contact us for more details.

Do you offer training?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include training sessions. We also provide comprehensive documentation and video tutorials for all users.
