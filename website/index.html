Why Choose Campaign Pro?

Everything you need to run a successful election campaign, all in one powerful platform

Voter Management

Import, organize, and manage your voter database with ease. Bulk import from CSV, search and filter voters by name, ID, or location (Male', Addu, Atolls). Track voter information efficiently.

Pledge Tracking

Track voter pledges and commitments across all wards (Henveiru, Galolhu, Machangolhi, etc.). Monitor pledge status, assign candidates, and analyze pledge data to measure campaign effectiveness.

Event Management

Schedule and manage campaign events. Track attendance, set reminders, and coordinate campaign activities with your team.

Call Management

Track all campaign calls made to voters. Record call status, notes, and follow-up actions to maintain comprehensive communication records.

Agent Management

Assign and manage field agents. Generate unique access links, track agent activities, and monitor voter assignments for better coordination.

Real-time Messaging

Communicate with your team in real-time. Built-in messaging system for instant collaboration and coordination during campaign activities.
